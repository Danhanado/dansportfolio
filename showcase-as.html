Action Spaceman is a personal video game and multimedia concept put together for NCEA Level 3. I designed characters, logos, branding assets, Game UI/Website mockups, 3D models/renders, posters and even a small proof-of-concept game demo.

To produce the projects 3D graphics, I taught myself how to use Cinema 4D and developed a unique rendering style using the material system.
