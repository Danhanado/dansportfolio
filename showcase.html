VirtuaCadeGUI (2021-2024)

A WIP GUI for PCs and handheld devices that allows an entire library of games to be sorted, categorised and launched from one location using a gamepad, keyboard, arcade stick and other input methods. Designed originally for a custom arcade cabinet project, this GUI is responsive, colourful and designed for easy readability on small and large screens.

Action Spaceman (2017)

An in-depth video game and franchise concept put together for an NCEA Level 3 external and scholarship, I designed characters, logos, branding assets, Game UI/Website mockups, 3D models/renders, posters and even a small proof-of-concept game demo.

Ticking Time-Trials (2020)

A small game demo where you must successfully pull-off heists by making a mad dash to the bank before you explode, evading obstacles and collecting loose change along the way.
